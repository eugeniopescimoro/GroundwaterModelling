Ingeniería sin Fronteras

The collaboration with Ingeniería sin Fronteras ( Argentinan chapter ) was developed around various field sites. In the metropolitan area of Buenos Aires I partecipated to the on-site construction of a kindergarden and a dining hall for people living in degradeted areas with limited resources. The last project I worked on during my three months stay was water storage project in a rural area in the north of Argentina (Colonia Dora).
